What is Homunkulus ?

Is your data just lying around on your computer, but at the same time important to you?

If you answered yes, we have the right solution for you. Our solution is called Homunkulus. This is a backup program that makes a 1 to 1 copy of your most important data and stores it wherever you want and the best part is that it is 100% free and open source. With Homunculus, your system is protected from unexpected crashes or malware.
  • Rescue in case of system crashes and attack by malware / ransomware
  • Data backup for maximum protection in case of hardware problems
  • Easy creation of backup plans
  • Restore data
  • Backup of files, partitions and more
  • Incremental backup that conserves resources
  • Backup to local and external hard disks and LAN
  • Open Source
